# Functional Scope

## Project Name

AI-Powered Customer Support & Service Automation

## Purpose

This project defines a Salesforce-centered customer service solution that combines intelligent automation, case management, and AI-assisted support experiences. The objective is to create a realistic and production-oriented portfolio project that reflects how modern service teams operate in enterprise environments.

## Business Goal

The solution aims to improve customer support efficiency by reducing manual effort, accelerating case resolution, and enabling agents to focus on high-value customer interactions.

## Target Users

- Support Agents
- Support Managers
- Customer Success Teams
- Service Operations Leads
- Business Stakeholders

## In Scope

### 1. Case Management

- Create, update, and track support cases.
- Route cases based on business rules and service priorities.
- Manage case status, escalation, and ownership.
- Support multi-channel case intake and follow-up.

### 2. AI-Assisted Support

- Intelligent case triage and categorization.
- Suggested routing based on issue type and customer profile.
- Recommendations for relevant knowledge articles and next-best actions.
- Automated prioritization of urgent or high-impact cases.

### 3. Service Automation

- Trigger-based workflow automation for routine support actions.
- Standardized case handling for common customer issues.
- Automated notifications, reminders, and task creation.
- SLA monitoring and escalation handling.

### 4. Knowledge and Self-Service Enablement

- Connect support cases to curated knowledge content.
- Support agent productivity with recommended articles.
- Provide a foundation for future self-service and chatbot integration.

### 5. Reporting and Oversight

- Dashboards for case volume, resolution trends, and SLA performance.
- Supervisor visibility into operational bottlenecks.
- Metrics for service quality and automation effectiveness.

## Out of Scope

The initial phase does not include:

- full production deployment,
- real external AI model integration,
- complete end-to-end customer portal implementation,
- complex third-party system integrations,
- or full enterprise compliance and security configuration.

## Assumptions

- The solution will be implemented using Salesforce as the core service platform.
- The repository will be structured in a way that supports future development.
- Documentation and solution design will be prioritized alongside implementation.
- The project is intended for portfolio and architectural demonstration purposes.

## Success Criteria

The project will be considered successful when it demonstrates:

- a clear and professional Salesforce repository structure,
- strong functional documentation,
- realistic service automation scenarios,
- and a compelling presentation of enterprise support capabilities.

## Future Enhancements

Future iterations may expand into:

- conversational AI and chatbot support,
- deeper CRM integration,
- advanced analytics and predictive service insights,
- and customer experience personalization.
