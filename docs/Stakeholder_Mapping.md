# Stakeholder Mapping

## Project Overview

AI-Powered Customer Support & Service Automation is a Salesforce-focused solution designed to modernize customer service operations through intelligent case handling, automated workflows, and AI-assisted support capabilities. The project aims to improve response quality, reduce manual effort, and create a scalable service experience for both customers and internal teams.

## Stakeholders

### 1. Customer
- Role: End user of the support service.
- Responsibilities: Submit support requests, provide issue details, and follow up on case progress.
- Goals: Receive fast, accurate, and transparent support.
- Pain Points: Long response times, repeated follow-ups, unclear case status.
- Benefits from the System: Faster issue resolution, better communication, and improved service consistency.

### 2. Customer Support Agent
- Role: Frontline support representative handling customer cases.
- Responsibilities: Manage daily cases, update case details, use recommended knowledge articles, and collaborate with other teams.
- Goals: Resolve cases efficiently and improve customer satisfaction.
- Pain Points: Repetitive tasks, fragmented information, high case volume.
- Benefits from the System: Reduced manual effort, guided recommendations, and improved productivity.

### 3. Support Manager
- Role: Operational leader overseeing support performance.
- Responsibilities: Monitor team performance, review escalations, and ensure service standards are met.
- Goals: Improve team productivity, maintain SLA compliance, and enhance service quality.
- Pain Points: Limited visibility into operational bottlenecks and inconsistent case handling.
- Benefits from the System: Better reporting, faster decision-making, and improved team oversight.

### 4. Salesforce Administrator
- Role: Technical owner of Salesforce configuration and platform maintenance.
- Responsibilities: Configure objects, flows, permissions, reports, and system settings.
- Goals: Maintain a stable and scalable Salesforce environment.
- Pain Points: Frequent change requests, unclear requirements, and coordination challenges.
- Benefits from the System: A structured solution that reduces rework and supports long-term maintainability.

### 5. Service Team
- Role: Cross-functional support operations group.
- Responsibilities: Define service processes, support adoption, and improve operational workflows.
- Goals: Standardize service excellence and improve readiness for changing business needs.
- Pain Points: Process inconsistency, manual workarounds, and siloed operations.
- Benefits from the System: Standardized workflows, better visibility, and improved collaboration.

### 6. AI Agent (Agentforce)
- Role: Digital assistance layer that supports intelligent recommendations and automation.
- Responsibilities: Assist with case triage, provide contextual guidance, and support automated service actions.
- Goals: Deliver accurate, timely, and context-aware support assistance.
- Pain Points: Poor data quality, unclear prompts, and limited business context.
- Benefits from the System: Increased accuracy, faster response generation, and better support outcomes.

### 7. Business Owner
- Role: Executive sponsor or business stakeholder for the initiative.
- Responsibilities: Define business goals, approve priorities, and ensure alignment with organizational strategy.
- Goals: Improve efficiency, customer experience, and service outcomes.
- Pain Points: Slow delivery, unclear ROI, and poor adoption.
- Benefits from the System: Greater operational efficiency, measurable service improvement, and stronger business value.

### 8. System Administrator
- Role: Platform administrator responsible for user access, system health, and core administration tasks.
- Responsibilities: Manage user permissions, security settings, and platform operations.
- Goals: Maintain secure and reliable system operations.
- Pain Points: Manual administrative overhead and changing support requirements.
- Benefits from the System: Cleaner administration processes and better control over platform behavior.

### 9. IT Support Team
- Role: Technical support group responsible for environment stability and issue resolution.
- Responsibilities: Support integrations, troubleshoot incidents, and coordinate technical fixes.
- Goals: Ensure platform reliability and minimize downtime.
- Pain Points: Complex incidents, unclear ownership, and delayed issue resolution.
- Benefits from the System: Better-defined workflows, fewer recurring issues, and improved handoff processes.

## Stakeholder Risks

- Lack of user adoption.
- Insufficient training and change readiness.
- Frequent requirement changes during delivery.
- Delayed approvals from business stakeholders.
- Poor communication across teams.
- Incomplete understanding of AI-driven recommendations.

## Mitigation Strategies

- Provide structured training and role-based enablement.
- Conduct early stakeholder engagement and feedback sessions.
- Maintain a clear change control process for requirement updates.
- Define approval milestones and escalation paths.
- Establish regular communication checkpoints with all key stakeholders.
- Validate AI behavior with business scenarios and user feedback before broad rollout.
